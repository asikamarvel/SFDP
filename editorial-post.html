SFDP Launches Health Innovation Challenge 2025 for Students and Young Professionals

Dr. Kennedy Alajemba
Founder & President
January 15, 2025 5 min read

The Society for Disease Prevention is thrilled to announce the launch of the SFDP Health Innovation Challenge 2025, a groundbreaking initiative designed to empower students and young professionals to develop innovative solutions for pressing global health challenges.

About the Challenge

In an era where healthcare systems worldwide face unprecedented challenges, we believe that the next generation of innovators holds the key to transformative solutions. The SFDP Health Innovation Challenge invites creative minds from around the world to propose innovative ideas that address critical health issues affecting underserved communities.

This year's challenge focuses on three key areas: disease prevention technologies, community health education solutions, and accessible healthcare delivery models. Participants will have the opportunity to develop their ideas with mentorship from healthcare professionals and public health experts.

Who Can Participate

The Innovation Challenge is open to:

  • Undergraduate and graduate students in any field of study
  • Young professionals (ages 18-35) working in healthcare, technology, or related sectors
  • Teams of up to 4 members with diverse backgrounds and expertise
  • Individuals from any country around the world

We particularly encourage participation from individuals in developing countries and those who have firsthand experience with the health challenges they aim to address.

"Innovation doesn't require expensive labs or advanced degrees. Sometimes the most impactful solutions come from those closest to the problems they're solving. We're excited to see what young minds will bring to this challenge."

Prizes and Opportunities

Winners will receive more than just recognition. The SFDP Health Innovation Challenge offers substantial support to help turn winning ideas into reality:

  • First Place: $5,000 seed funding + mentorship program + opportunity to pilot the solution
  • Second Place: $2,500 seed funding + mentorship program
  • Third Place: $1,000 seed funding + mentorship program
  • All Finalists: Certificate of recognition, SFDP network access, and potential internship opportunities

Important Dates

Mark your calendars for these key dates:

  • January 15, 2025: Application portal opens
  • March 31, 2025: Application deadline
  • April 15, 2025: Finalists announced
  • May 1-15, 2025: Mentorship and refinement period
  • May 30, 2025: Final presentations and winners announced

How to Apply

Ready to make a difference? Applying is simple:

  1. Visit our Innovation Challenge page and create an account
  2. Complete the online application form with your personal information
  3. Submit a brief proposal (max 1,500 words) outlining your innovative solution
  4. Include a 3-minute video pitch explaining your idea and its potential impact
  5. Provide any supporting materials or prototypes (optional)

Applications are reviewed by a panel of healthcare professionals, entrepreneurs, and public health experts who evaluate submissions based on innovation, feasibility, scalability, and potential impact.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the Innovation Challenge, please don't hesitate to reach out. Our team is here to help you through the application process.

Contact us at innovation@s-fdp.org or visit our Innovation Challenge page for more details.

Ready to Change the World?

Applications are now open. Don't miss this opportunity to make a real impact on global health.
