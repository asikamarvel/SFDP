About The Challenge

Innovate For Global Health

The SFDP Health Innovation Challenge is a global competition that invites creative minds to develop solutions for pressing health challenges affecting underserved communities worldwide.

Whether you're a student, recent graduate, or young professional, this is your opportunity to make a real impact. We're looking for innovative ideas that can prevent diseases, improve healthcare access, and educate communities.

Challenge Tracks

Choose Your Focus Area

Select one of three tracks that align with your expertise and passion. Each track addresses critical global health challenges.

Disease Prevention Technology

Develop innovative technologies, tools, or systems that help prevent the spread of infectious diseases in communities.

  • Early warning systems
  • Diagnostic tools
  • Sanitation solutions
  • Vaccination tracking

Community Health Education

Create engaging educational resources, platforms, or programs that effectively communicate health information to diverse communities.

  • Mobile apps
  • Interactive content
  • Awareness campaigns
  • Training programs

Healthcare Access Models

Design innovative service delivery models that improve healthcare access for underserved and remote populations.

  • Telemedicine solutions
  • Mobile clinics
  • Community health workers
  • Supply chain innovation

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Requirements

The Innovation Challenge is open to passionate individuals who want to make a difference in global health.

  • Students

    Undergraduate or graduate students in any field of study

  • Young Professionals

    Ages 18-35 working in healthcare, tech, or related fields

  • Teams Welcome

    Teams of up to 4 members with diverse backgrounds

  • Global Participation

    Open to individuals from any country worldwide

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Applications are submitted through our online portal. You'll need to create an account, fill out the application form, submit a written proposal (max 1,500 words), and upload a 3-minute video pitch explaining your idea.

Both individuals and teams can apply. Teams can have up to 4 members. We encourage diverse teams with complementary skills, but solo applicants are equally welcome.

No, there is no application fee. The SFDP Health Innovation Challenge is completely free to enter.

No, a working prototype is not required for the initial application. We're looking for innovative ideas with strong potential. Finalists will have the opportunity to develop their concepts further during the mentorship period.

Submissions are evaluated based on: Innovation (30%), Feasibility (25%), Scalability (20%), Potential Impact (20%), and Presentation Quality (5%). Our panel includes healthcare professionals, entrepreneurs, and public health experts.

Winners receive their prize money, begin the mentorship program, and may have the opportunity to pilot their solution through SFDP's programs. We're committed to helping turn winning ideas into reality.
