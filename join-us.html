Why Join Us

Make A Real Impact

At SFDP, we're a 100% volunteer-run organization. Every member of our team contributes their time and skills because they believe in our mission to prevent disease and save lives.

Whether you have a few hours a week or can commit to regular involvement, your contribution makes a difference. Join a community of passionate individuals working together for global health.

  • Flexible remote and on-site opportunities
  • Professional development and training
  • Networking with healthcare professionals
  • Certificate of volunteer service

Get Started

Apply To Volunteer

Ready to make a difference? Fill out the application form and tell us about yourself. We'll match you with opportunities that fit your skills and interests.

What Happens Next?

  1. Submit your application
  2. Our team reviews your submission (1-2 weeks)
  3. Schedule an introductory call
  4. Complete volunteer orientation
  5. Start making an impact!

Questions?
