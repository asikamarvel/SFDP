AIDS & Rapid Disease Screening

We expand HIV testing, awareness, and rapid diagnostic services across African communities, promoting prevention, early detection, and access to treatment in high-risk and underserved areas. Early detection saves lives, and we're committed to making healthcare accessible to those who need it most.
